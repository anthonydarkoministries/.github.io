Our Vision

Welcome to ANTHONY DARKO MINISTRIES (SPIRIT GENERATION CHURCH - BETH ELOHIM). We believe in the gifts and power of the Holy Spirit to teach and to preach the Gospel of the Kingdom and to share the love of Jesus - Yeshua with all people. We desire nothing from this world but to be in the presence of our Heavenly Father and to gaze at His beauty all the days of our lives.

"Having abolished in his flesh the enmity, even the law of commandments contained in ordinances; for to make in himself of twain one new man, so making peace;" - Ephesians 2:15 (KJV)

"After this I beheld, and, lo, a great multitude, which no man could number, of all nations, and kindreds, and people, and tongues, stood before the throne, and before the Lamb..." - Revelation 7:9 (KJV)

Our Calling and Passion

Our calling is to build the Kingdom of God by bringing the church, the bride of Jesus Christ, into a deeper relationship with her Bridegroom through intimacy of worship and prayer, teaching of the Word, the Prophetic, and uniting the church as one people of God and one new man in Christ Yeshua. We obey the new commandment that we love each other as Jesus Christ loved us, so that the world would know that we are His disciples. We actively disciple the next generation of spiritual leaders to be commissioned and sent into the world for the harvest. And we prepare the church for the imminent return of her Bridegroom.

"Many people shall be joined to the LORD in that day, and they shall become My people. And I will dwell in your midst. Then you will know that the LORD of Hosts has sent me to you." - Zechariah 2:11 (KJV)

About the Founder

Anthony Darko is a humble and dedicated minister of the Gospel of God, called with an Apostolic and Prophetic mantle for this generation. Rooted in the Word of Truth — the gospel of our salvation as declared in Ephesians 1:13 — he believes deeply that all who hear and receive this message are sealed by the promised Holy Spirit. With a burning passion for the presence of God and a heart for His people, he labours tirelessly to see the Church walk in her true identity and power. He is a worshipper, a teacher, and a spiritual father, committed to raising up sons and daughters who will carry the fire of revival to the ends of the earth.

My Daughter My Child Foundation

Empowering the next generation of prominent women in society and the Kingdom.

The "My Daughter My Child Foundation" is a heartfelt initiative of ANTHONY DARKO MINISTRIES focused on the girl child. We believe that every young girl is a precious gift, filled with immense potential to become a powerful and influential leader.

Our mission is to take very good care of the girl child, providing a safe, nurturing, and empowering environment. We are currently targeting young girls from the ages of 5 to 18 years, offering mentorship, education support, spiritual guidance, and life skills training.

By sowing into this foundation, you are helping to shape a brighter future for these young women, equipping them to overcome challenges, discover their purpose, and become the prominent figures God has called them to be in society and in His Kingdom.
